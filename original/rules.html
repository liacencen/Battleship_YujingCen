Game Rules

How to Play

  1. ⚓ Each player has a fleet of ships placed randomly on a 10x10 grid.
  2. 🔥 Players take turns selecting grid coordinates to fire at the enemy’s board.
  3. ✅ Hits are marked with ✅, and misses are marked with ❌.
  4. 🚢 The goal is to sink all of the enemy's ships before they sink yours.
  5. 🏆 The game continues until one player has no ships remaining.
